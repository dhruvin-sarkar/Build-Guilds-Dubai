---

## What This Was

Build Guild Dubai was a local satellite event of [Hack Club Blueprint](https://blueprint.hackclub.com) a global program by [Hack Club](https://hackclub.com) where teens aged 13–18 design and build hardware projects and get up to **$400 in funding** to make them real.

From October 2025 to March 2026, Blueprint funded **1,500 hardware projects** built by **1,000 teenagers across 77 countries**, giving out over **$140,000 in grants**. Projects range from custom PCBs and mechanical keyboards to audio visualizers, robotics, and more  all built and shipped by teens.

## What Was Happening on the Day

* Intro to Hardware — crash course on PCBs, firmware, embedded systems
* Components Crash Course + Hands-On Build Time
* Lunch (included)
* Show & Tell — bring your hardware projects
* PCB Workshop — design a hacker card or keychain in EasyEDA / KiCad
* Intro to Hack Club, Blueprint & Hackpad — how to get funded for your own builds
* Open Build Time + Wind Down

## Cancellation Note

Hey everyone,

Because of what's going on in Dubai right now, it has been very hard to find a place to hold the event. I contacted cafes, makerspaces, public places, colleges, universities, and schools, but most of them said they could not host because of the current situation. Aly has been very helpful the whole time, but unfortunately he has not been able to stay because of exams.

We were holding on to some hope with the ceasefire, but we had almost everything in place except for the final details about the venue and the BOM. Shipping has been very hard lately, and with the current airspace restrictions and the high costs and delays involved in getting the BOM from JLCPCB or PCBWay, it has not been possible to get everything delivered on time. I also talked to the JLCPCB group, which includes OSHWA Labs and EasyEDA, and looked into a counter-sponsorship with PCBWay, but sadly none of this could come together in a way that would work for the event in the time we had.

It is not realistic anymore because I have SATs on May 2nd and I still need time to find a place, get everything ready, and plan everything to the level we had in mind. I do not want to put together something quickly or poorly, especially since Aly and I were both really looking forward to making this event special.

This is hard to say after being let down by the cancellation of Dubai Campfire, which we were both looking forward to even more so for Aly since he put so much effort into organizing it at the beginning of everything, but I think the best thing to do right now is to call off the event.

Thanks to everyone for being patient and helpful during this. It really means a lot.
